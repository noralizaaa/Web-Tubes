About our store

A one-stop center for pet needs and facilities located in Alam Sutera, offering a wide range of services such as a pet shop, grooming, hotel for dogs and cats, pool & playground, clinic, fish & reptile corner, and dog training.

Currently, Pet Kingdom operates 9 stores across Jabodetabek, Bandung, Surabaya, and Bali, providing top-quality products and professional services for pets and their owners. With a commitment to ensuring the well-being and happiness of pets, Pet Kingdom has become the ultimate destination for pet lovers in Indonesia.
